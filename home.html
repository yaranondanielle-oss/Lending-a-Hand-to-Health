WHAT IS HEALTH?

Health is to be defined by a holistic well-being for your physical, mental, and emotional state. We underline holistic here as health isn’t merely the absence of injury or ill, it is upholding each one of your physical, mental, and emotional aspects. Healthcare is a societal good that seeks to maintain and uphold “health”. Every day, every right decision you make, that is health and that is healthcare.
